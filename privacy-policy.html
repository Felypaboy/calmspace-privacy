Privacy Policy – CalmSpace

Last updated: April 22, 2026

CalmSpace ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application CalmSpace ("the App") handles information.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

CalmSpace does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The App works fully offline and does not require an account or registration.

We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers beyond what AdMob requires
  • Usage data or analytics beyond what AdMob requires

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. This may include:

  • Advertising ID (GAID)
  • IP address
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Ad interaction data

For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy and AdMob's data collection practices.

3. Children's Privacy

CalmSpace is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

4. Data Storage

All app preferences (such as volume settings and timer selections) are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted to us or any third party.

5. Third-Party Services

The App integrates Google AdMob. We encourage you to review Google's privacy policy to understand how they handle data.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
